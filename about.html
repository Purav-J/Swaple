About Us

Since childhood, we, as siblings, have shared an immense love for furry little babies. Every time we saw a stray dog or a wandering cat, we dreamt of adopting them all and giving them a loving home. Of course, that wasn’t possible, but we somehow convinced our parents to let us adopt and we got two cats, who quickly became our family. However, that wasn’t enough for us—we knew our love for animals extended far beyond our home.

That’s when Swaple was born—not just as a pet shop but as a place where love, care and passion for animals come together. The name Swaple itself is inspired by our beloved cats, Sweetie and Maple, who taught us the true meaning of companionship and unconditional love. We believe that every pet deserves the best, and our mission is to provide top-quality care, nutritious food, and essential products that enhance the lives of pets and their owners. Whether you're a first-time pet parent or a lifelong animal lover, we are here to support you on your journey of giving your pet a happy and healthy life.

Why Choose Us

Swaple is your one-stop destination for premium pet products. We understand the bond you share with your furry babies and are committed to offering the best food. Our team of pet lovers and professionals ensures that every product meets the highest standards of quality and safety.
